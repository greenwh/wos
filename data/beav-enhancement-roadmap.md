## Beav vs. Wally: Key Differences That Shape This Roadmap

Beav is a strictly F2P account, 10 hero levels behind Wally, and generally 1–2 phases behind in gear and skill progression. But there are bright spots — Flint's Mythic gear transfer from Sergey landed well, and Molly's priority pieces already have strong MF levels despite lower raw enhancement. The strategy here is about being disciplined with scarce resources: fewer Skill Manuals, no spending shortcuts, and a tighter prioritization funnel.

**What Beav does well:** Flint's Mythic Gloves/Belt at +49 MF3 are solid. Molly has MF3 Goggles and MF2 Boots — her MF levels are actually ahead of Wally's Molly. Stand at Arms on Jessie is already Lv4.

**Where Beav is behind:** Bahiti is still on Epic priority pieces (no Mythic). Jessie has Rare gear in three slots. Multiple heroes have skills stuck at Lv2–3 where Wally's are at Lv4. Alonzo is at 0★ — even further from roster-ready than Wally's.

---

## Key Principle: Where Your Skills Actually Apply

**Arena and Exploration stages → Exploration skills.** Hero-vs-hero battles where skill levels directly scale damage, defense, and survivability.

**Labyrinth, Garrison, Rally, AC, Foundry Battle → Expedition skills.** Troop-based combat where Expedition skills buff army stats. The backbone of competitive PvP.

Both matter. On a F2P account with limited manuals, sequencing them correctly matters even more.

## Phase 1: Immediate Priorities (Next 2–4 Weeks)

### Gear: Push Flint's Priority Pieces to +55

Flint's Gloves and Belt are at Mythic +49 MF3 — already strong, but pushing toward +55 is the best return on Ore you'll find. The MF3 is done; now it's about raw enhancement to squeeze more stats.

**Action items:**
- Push Flint's **Gloves from +49 to +55**. The stat gains per enhancement level are still strong in this range.
- Push Flint's **Belt from +49 to +55** in parallel. Keep them roughly even — no reason to max one while the other lags.
- Don't push past +55 right now. Diminishing returns hit hard above +55, and Molly's pieces need the Ore more urgently.

### Gear: Push Molly's Priority Pieces Higher

Molly's Goggles (+29 MF3) and Boots (+32 MF2) are the biggest gear opportunity on Beav. The MF levels are excellent for where this account is — MF3 on Goggles is ahead of Wally's Molly — but the raw enhancement is low. This is where your Ore should go after Flint.

**Action items:**
- Push Molly's **Boots from +32 to +40** minimum. Boots are slightly ahead of Goggles in enhancement, and the stat jump from +32 to +40 is substantial.
- Push Molly's **Goggles from +29 to +38** minimum. Closing the gap between these two pieces keeps her stat profile balanced.
- Push Molly's **Boots MF to MF3** when Essence Stones allow. Getting both priority pieces to MF3 gives Beav's Molly a MF advantage over most Gen 2 F2P players.

### Skills: Flint's Expedition Skills to Lv4

Flint's Expedition skills are a mixed bag: Burning Resolve is at Lv4 (great — that's the money skill), but Pyromaniac and Immolation are both stuck at Lv3. For Labyrinth and troop combat, getting the full Expedition suite to Lv4 matters.

**Action items:**
- Level **Pyromaniac to Lv4** first — sustained troop damage buff in Expedition content.
- Level **Immolation to Lv4** next — completes the set.
- **Heat Diffusion (Exploration) to Lv3** as a low-cost side item — it's stuck at Lv2 while his other Exploration skills are Lv4. The Lv2→3 jump is cheap and removes a weak link in Arena.

### Skills: Bahiti's Exploration Skills to Lv4

Same situation as Wally — Bahiti's Exploration skills are all at Lv3 (Precise Shot, Quick Shot, Pathfinder Vision). This is the single biggest Arena improvement available on Beav right now.

**Action items:**
- Level **Precise Shot to Lv4** first — primary damage.
- Then **Quick Shot to Lv4** — secondary damage.
- Then **Pathfinder Vision to Lv4** — utility.
- This is the highest-impact manual spend on the Beav account for Arena. Do this before anything else skill-related.

## Phase 2: Mid-Term Improvements (Weeks 4–8)

### Gear: Bahiti's Mythic Upgrade Path

Bahiti's priority pieces (Goggles and Boots for Marksman) are still at Epic +48. On Wally, Bahiti already has Mythic. Beav needs to start the Mythic transition through Arena tokens.

**Action items:**
- Acquire **Mythic Goggles** for Bahiti via Arena tokens. This is the grind — prioritize Goggles over Boots since Goggles provide Hero Attack + Health.
- Once acquired, enhance to **+30–35** range. Don't over-invest — remember these eventually transfer to Alonzo.
- The Epic +48 pieces become hand-me-downs for Gina or stay as banked value.
- **Mythic Boots** for Bahiti are the second Arena token target after Goggles.

### Gear: Jessie Needs Epic Upgrades

Jessie's gear situation is Beav's weakest point: Rare Boots (+33), Rare Gloves, and Rare Belt. For a hero whose entire value is Stand at Arms (Lv4) in Expedition content, she needs at least Epic priority pieces.

**Action items:**
- Acquire **Epic Boots** for Jessie and enhance to +40 range. Boots are a Lancer priority piece. Replacing Rare +33 with Epic +40 is a big jump.
- Acquire **Epic Gloves and Belt** when drops allow — even unenhanced Epic is a meaningful upgrade over Rare.
- Jessie's Goggles (Epic +41) are fine for now. That's her best piece.
- Don't Mythic anything for Jessie on Beav. The resources are better spent on Flint, Molly, and Bahiti.

### Skills: Molly's Expedition Gap

Molly's Expedition skills have a gap: Snow's Grace is at Lv3 while Ice Dominion and Youthful Rage are at Lv4. Snow's Grace is her primary troop buff — it's the one that matters most for Labyrinth and troop combat.

**Action items:**
- Level **Snow's Grace to Lv4** — this is higher priority than most other skill investments. It's Molly's core Expedition skill and it's a level behind her others.
- Level **Youthful Persistence (Exploration) to Lv4** when manuals allow — it's at Lv3 while her other Exploration skills are Lv4. Completes her Arena set.

### Skills: Gina's Expedition Skills

Same issue as Wally — Gina's Expedition skills are Endurance Training Lv2 and Quick Paced Lv2. Both at Lv2 is a real liability for any troop content she participates in.

**Action items:**
- Level both **Endurance Training and Quick Paced to Lv3**. The Lv2→3 jump is cheap and both are equally important.
- Don't push past Lv3 on either — Gina is a secondary hero with limited long-term value.

### Stars: Push Bahiti and Sergey to 4★

Both are at 3★ with 5/6 slivers — one sliver each from 4★. Epic hero shards come through regular gameplay (Intel Missions, Recruitment, Trials). These are free stats and should happen naturally.

**Action items:**
- Get Bahiti's final sliver → **4★**. Opens Lv5 skill potential on Sixth Sense.
- Get Sergey's final sliver → **4★**. Modest benefit for his garrison role but free is free.
- Gina at 3★ 4/6 will follow shortly through the same channels.

## Phase 4: Alonzo Transition (Weeks 12–20)

### The Long F2P Road

Alonzo on Beav is at 0★ with 1/6 slivers — even further from roster-ready than Wally's 1★. On a F2P account, the path to a usable Alonzo is longer, which means Bahiti stays as your primary Marksman for a good while yet.

**The transition plan:**

**Step 1 — Accumulate shards patiently.** Hall of Heroes and Daily Deals are the paths. Target **3★ minimum** before even thinking about activation. On pure F2P, this likely takes 3–4 Lucky Wheel cycles plus consistent Daily Deal purchases. Don't rush it.

**Step 2 — Pre-invest in skills (sparingly).** When manual supply allows and your active roster skills are at Lv4:
- **Trapnet to Lv3** — signature AoE + immobilize.
- **Onslaught to Lv3** — primary Expedition troop buff.
- Don't go beyond Lv3 until Alonzo is actually replacing Bahiti. Manuals are more scarce on Beav.

**Step 3 — Gear transfer.** When the time comes, Bahiti's Mythic Goggles and Boots (once you've acquired them in Phase 2) transfer directly to Alonzo. Same troop type = free. This is why Bahiti's Mythic upgrade path matters even though he's a placeholder.

**Step 4 — Weapon.** Alonzo has Captain Ahab already. Widget upgrades come through Enigma events and the Mystery Shop — grab them when available. The 15% rally lethality from Harpoon Enhancement is game-changing once unlocked.

## Phase 5: Lv5 Skill Pushes and Endgame Gear (Ongoing)

### Skill Lv5 Targets (Requires 4★+ Heroes)

On F2P, Lv5 pushes are expensive and far out. Be very selective — target only the skills with the biggest impact-per-manual.

**Jessie — Stand at Arms Lv5** (Expedition): The 25% all-troop damage buff at max. Jessie is already at 4★ via Epic shard availability, making this the most accessible Lv5 target on Beav. This is probably your first Lv5 push.

**Flint — Burning Resolve Lv5** (Expedition): 25% troop attack buff. Requires 4★ which is gated by event shards — longer road for a Mythic hero on F2P. But this is the #1 long-term target.

**Molly — Snow's Grace Lv5** (Expedition): Primary Lancer troop buff. Same 4★ gate as Flint. Target after Burning Resolve.

### Endgame Gear Targets

**Flint's secondary pieces:** Mythic Goggles and Boots via Arena tokens, same grind as Wally. Enhancement target: +30–35 range.

**Chief Gear (Furnace 22):** Beav is further from Furnace 22, so this is further out. When it arrives: Infantry (Jacket/Pants) → Marksman (Ring/Cane) → Lancer (Helmet/Watch). Craft all six pieces first, enhance evenly in sets of 3 for set bonuses.

## What NOT to Spend Resources On

- **Sergey's gear beyond Epic +45.** His Gloves/Belt are serviceable for garrison. Don't Mythic anything for Sergey — those resources go to Flint and Bahiti.
- **Jessie's Mythic gear.** On F2P, Mythic tokens are too scarce. Epic is her ceiling on Beav. Save Mythic resources for Flint, Molly, and Bahiti (→ Alonzo).
- **Gina's Exploration skills past Lv3.** She's a secondary hero. Lv3 is enough for her Arena contribution on Beav.
- **Bench hero skills.** Zinman, Seo-yoon, Ling Xue, Lumak Bokan, and others stay as-is. Every manual counts on F2P.
- **Reforging Bahiti's Epic +48 pieces.** When he gets Mythic replacements, the Epic pieces become hand-me-downs for Gina or just banked value. Reforging at +48 is wasteful.
- **Alonzo skills beyond Lv3** until he's actually replacing Bahiti. Manuals are too precious on this account to pre-invest heavily.
- **Spreading MF across many pieces.** Focus MF investment one piece at a time. Molly's Boots to MF3 before touching anything else.
